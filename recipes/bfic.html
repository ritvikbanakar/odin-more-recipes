Recipe for Cinnamon Fried Bananas

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas peeled
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Cut the bananas into 1/2" inch thick slices
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, melt the butter. Whisk in the honey, lemon juice, cinnamon, and salt to combine.
  3. Add the banana slices, and pan fry for 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden and caramelized all over. Lower heat if they brown too quickly.
  4. Serve warm over ice cream or on top of fluffy pancakes or waffles.
